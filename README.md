## Mission

**To build, repair, and restore lives for many generations.**
*Isaiah 58:12*

> “Your people will rebuild the ancient ruins and will raise up the age-old foundations; you will be called Repairer of Broken Walls, Restorer of Streets with Dwellings.” *(NIV)*
> “And they from among you shall build the old waste places…” *(KJV)*
> “And your ancient ruins shall be rebuilt…” *(Amplified)*

Our mission is to build broken lives and minds using the Word of God through:

* Reaching out with a transforming message to the incapacitated
* Serving people
* Equipping and building people

## Platform Scope (MVP)

This repository powers the first phase of our digital platform:

* Sunday Music Department Evaluation
* Structured data capture for continuous improvement
* Scalable backend for future training, production, and capacity building
